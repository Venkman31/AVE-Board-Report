Managing Directors Executive Summary & Strategic Context

We start the first financial period of the year with a clear focus on 3 key pillars of the business. People. Foundations & Growth.

  • People - We have held a strategic away day with the whole business in which we increased the teams understanding of our vision & values with avg scoring increasing substantially from a 2.7/5 to a 4.5/5. The team also delivered departmental updates and client service excellence training sessions to align everyone in each department with key projects. The away day also engaged with our L&D programme with presentations from their team to our whole business to ensure everyone has the opportunity to thrive. We are also expanding capacity within our office environment to account for the additional head count of 2 new buyers we have invested in and ensure they become part of our office and business culture with a place to call home.
  • Foundations - Work on our internal and client facing systems is increasing significantly with improvements to Procure Wizard as well as Halo and our ticketing and Sales platforms to streamline processes, increase efficiencies and drive data output. Significant investment into the Paying the Bill project which we expect to receive a ‘Go/NoGo’ decision from the Project Team on 19th Nov 25.
  • Growth - Our priority has been winning new business in month (3wins including our first hotel group) and building the Go To Market Strategy and Operational Processes for Sales, Marketing, PR & SEO and Onboarding new business. These are already being implemented within the business and recruitment underway for 2 new BDM’s with strong KPI expectations.
